<section class="section">
  <div class="container">
    <h2>Could Genetics Play a Role?</h2>
    <p class="prose">
      Most neurological concerns don't involve a genetic cause. A few
      situations are worth a closer look — if any of these sound
      familiar, it may be worth discussing:
    </p>
    <ul class="prose">
      <li>A neurological condition that runs in the family</li>
      <li>Symptoms that began earlier in life than typically expected</li>
      <li>A neurodegenerative condition without a clear cause identified so far</li>
      <li>Unexplained muscle weakness or a suspected muscular dystrophy</li>
      <li>A stroke at a young age, or without the usual risk factors</li>
      <li>A relative with a known genetic neurological diagnosis</li>
    </ul>
    <p><a href="{{ '/services/neurogenetics/' | relative_url }}">Learn more about the Neurogenetics Service &rarr;</a></p>
  </div>
</section>
